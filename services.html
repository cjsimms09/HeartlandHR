HR services for small businesses.

Every business leans on HR in a different way. We offer focused services you can use individually or together, depending on what your team needs most right now.

1
Payroll services

Support for payroll setup, routine processing, and year-end tasks so paychecks go out correctly and on time.

  • Payroll system setup and clean-up.
  • Ongoing payroll processing and review.
  • Coordination of taxes and year-end reporting.

Ideal for owners who want confidence that employees are paid accurately without watching every detail themselves.

2
Benefits enrollment & administration

A practical partner to guide you and your employees through open enrollment and ongoing benefits questions.

  • Open enrollment planning and employee communication.
  • Coordination with benefits carriers and brokers.
  • Help with eligibility changes and life events.

Ideal for teams that want benefits to be clear and organized, not confusing or overwhelming.

3
Compliant policies & procedures

Clear, practical HR documentation that supports managers and employees while keeping compliance in view.

  • Employee handbooks and core HR policies.
  • Standard operating procedures for routine HR tasks.
  • Updates when regulations or business needs change.

Ideal for businesses that have grown beyond a few employees and need HR paperwork to match reality.

4
Ongoing HR support

A flexible way to get HR questions answered and decisions documented without hiring a full-time HR role.

  • Day-to-day HR questions from owners and managers.
  • Guidance on documentation and performance conversations.
  • Referrals to other resources when you need a specialist.

Ideal for leaders who want a trusted HR partner they can call when something comes up.
