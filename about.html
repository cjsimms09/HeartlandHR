About Heartland HR Consulting.

Heartland HR exists to give small businesses a practical, people-first way to handle HR, payroll, and benefits without the heaviness of a large firm.

Madison Simms
Founder · Heartland HR Consulting

Based in Kansas · Serving small businesses across the Midwest

An HR partner who understands small business reality.

For more than 10 years, Madison has helped small employers navigate payroll, benefits, and everyday HR questions without the big-firm feel.

She has worked alongside owners who wear every hat in the business—answering HR questions, tightening up paperwork, and building simple processes that actually fit how teams work.

Heartland HR is built around listening first, then offering clear options so you can make confident decisions about your people, compliance, and growth.

How we show up for clients.

  • Plain-language explanations instead of HR jargon.
  • Right-sized processes that match the size and pace of your business.
  • Steady, calm support when something sensitive or urgent comes up.
  • Documentation and follow-up so decisions are captured, not just discussed.

The types of teams we serve.

  • Independent medical practices and clinics.
  • Local service businesses with growing teams.
  • Nonprofits that need HR support but not a full-time role.
  • Owners stepping into HR responsibilities for the first time.
